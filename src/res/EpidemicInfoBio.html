About The Epidemic Simulator

This simulator was created by APCS-A students Cole Dumas and Luke Strachan and inspired by Grant Sanderson of 3Blue1Brown. The project began April 17, 2020 during the COVID-19 Pandemic; v1.0 was released May 1, 2020. Currently beta testing v1.20 for a Fall 2021 release.
